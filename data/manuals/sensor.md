# Sensor Calibration Manual

## Temperature Sensor Recalibration
To recalibrate the industrial temperature probe (Model TP-2000):
1. Isolate the sensor from the active process line. Ensure the line is depressurized if applicable.
2. Prepare an ice bath (0°C) and a boiling water bath (100°C at sea level).
3. Submerge the probe in the ice bath. Wait 3 minutes for the reading to stabilize.
4. If the reading is not 0°C ±0.5°C, access the hidden calibration menu on the transmitter display by holding the MENU and ENTER buttons for 5 seconds.
5. Select "Zero Cal" and confirm.
6. Submerge the probe in the boiling water bath. Wait 3 minutes.
7. If the reading is not 100°C ±0.5°C, select "Span Cal" from the menu and adjust.
8. Verify readings again. Wait for the probe to reach room temperature before reinstalling.

## Pressure Sensor Troubleshooting
- **Erratic Readings:** Often caused by a loose wire connection at the terminal block or electromagnetic interference (EMI). Ensure shielded cables are properly grounded.
- **Drift:** Gradual drift over months is normal wear but sudden drift indicates diaphragm damage. Replace the sensor.
